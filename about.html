BEA WHO?

Bea is short for Maria Beatriz. I was born in Caracas, Venezuela in 1994. I am a graphic design student currently enrolled at the Miami International University of Art and Design. Ever since I was little I have always been interested in technology and the arts. I'm responsible, organized, proactive, enthusiastic and passionate about everything that I do. Up to today, my career has been an amazing journey and I can't wait for what more's in store for me.
